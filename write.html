Write a Letter

Interested in participating, but can't make it in person?

Then, you're in the right place! We've made a form that will collect your your typed letter and then email them to us. From then on, we will handwrite them and bring them to the right place. 

We'd love if you would treat this letter as if you were writing to someone in your own family, add some emotion.

Only enter it if you'd like to include it in your letter.
