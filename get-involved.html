Get Involved

In our FBLA Community Service Project and other events.

Getting involved in our FBLA Chapter is easy with the site.

You can get involved in our FBLA chapter by filling out forms, or emailing the included details on this site.

Currently Open Involvement Spots:
  • Community Service Project Volunteering
Because of our current focus on the Community Service Project, we are only offering volunteering positions. 
You can get involved in this in three ways:
  1. ​​​​​​​Learning more about the project and then filling out the forms on the homepage.
  2. Write a letter from the comfort of your home. The letter(s) you write will then be accompanied with the gift baskets delivered.
  3. Donate money to help our Community Service Efforts.

Alright, so what does each option do for the community?

Option 1: Learning more, and volunteering.
By learning more and volunteering, you directly help our Community Service Project by you enabling us to speed up the process, and deliver the gift baskets on time. 

Option 2: Writing a letter
By you writing a letter, either from the comfort of your home, office, or wherever, you are allowing our nursing homes to be able to Recieve more letters than they may have expected. If enough members write multiple letters, than each resident may be able to recieve two letters, compared to only one. This will definetly warm their hearts, when maybe they would've recieved zero letters/cards. It also presents to our community that our FBLA members care about local nursing home residents.

Option 3: Donate
Donating can help us in many ways. The way we have it setup is, you can donate with DonorsChoose. DonorsChoose has listed our Amazon lists, so you know exactly where your money is going towards. The list contain items that are going to help our project efforts, and also fulfil the request that one nursing home had requested. 

Okay, this sounds great! Where do I start?

As stated above, you can find all of our involvement options on our homepage. However, instead of making you go find them, we have include a few buttons for our three options. Feel free to click whichever option you would like to proceed with.
