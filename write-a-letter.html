Write a letter

Interested in participating, but can't make it in person, or want to do it online?

Well you're in luck. We have made a form where you can write a letter. The letters you write will be handwritten, then sent to a nursing home, alongside a gift basket. These letters that you write should contain some emotion. We'd like you to treat it as if you were writing to your own family member, add some emotion.

*Simply submitting your letter does not  guarantee that your letter will actually be delivered. Your letter must first be evaluated to make sure it is appropriate.
